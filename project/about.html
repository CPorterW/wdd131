My idea of an open-ended school project

I made this for a web development final as a sophomore at BYUI. The assignment was to create a website that is interesting or useful, so naturally I created something that is interesting to me, but probably not interesting to anyone else.

Porter Williams

That's my name! No, I'm not named after a short, squeaky, bad-A gunslinging bodyguard in our history, but I am named after my patriarch grandfather and great-grandfather, which in my estimation, is just as cool. I am married to the illustrious Danielle Williams who is pregnant with the equally illustrious Hudson C Williams. I am a software engineer-in-training living in sunny Arizona. I believe that we are made to be happy, that we will be happy, and that worshipping God is our quickest path to supreme happiness.
