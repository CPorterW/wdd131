A celebration of tradition and grit.

A weekly podcast and blog by Brett Mckay looks to experts of the past and present to discover ways to be better men. 1000+ weekly interviews and 4000+ articles discuss everything from how to throw a boomerang to what it's like to be santa. He is a strong proponent of the family.

AoM embodies our people.

Our religion is 200 years old, at least, if not 2000, if not since that fateful pre-mortal counsel, if not since God pulled us out of nothing into spiritual existence. Art of Manliness looks to old traditions for inspiration about the type of men we should be. As men receive a cultural backlash for millenia of being more aggressive, socially dominating, and lustful, our fragile-egoed species definitely needs a reason to be proud of who we are. AoM shows that we are more than what our culture tells us. We can be honorable, gentle, and generous.

Listen

I listen to AoM on spotify. The interviews are light-hearted and interesting, perfect for my drive to work. I don't take any of the advice as seriously as I could, but I learn some fun facts as well as manly and virtuous ways of thinking.
